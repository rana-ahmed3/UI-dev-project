Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

A bright and zesty roasted chicken that’s simple and satisfying.

Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
45 mins
Servings
4
Add to Meal Plan Add to Favorites

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3.5–4 lbs)
  • 2 lemons, halved
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat the chicken dry.
  2. Whisk oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Rub mixture over chicken; stuff cavity with lemon halves.
  4. Roast 45–55 minutes, until juices run clear and skin is crisp.
  5. Rest 10 minutes, carve, and serve with pan juices.
